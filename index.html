About me...

I'm Chemeka, and I'm a passionate and detail-oriented web developer, and I specialize in creating intuitive and responsive user experiences.

My journey into web development started in 2015 with basic JavaScript. I did not know where to start in my journey, so I started with the basic foundation of JavaScript. I had always had a fascination with computers, how they worked, and how I could bring my ideas to life. I eventually moved on to HTML and CSS, learning how to embed JavaScript into my code. With this knowledge came an understanding of Notepad++ and creating live sites. With some assistance, I quickly moved on to GitHub, and learned I could share my creative works with others. My thirst for knowledge continued to grow and eventually I learned BootStrap by reverse- engineering a friend's website. By reverse-engineering their code, I learned how crucial the right elements are to have and when creating classes and ID's, the important role each one plays.


What drives me the most about web development is creating my code from scratch and transforming any complex challenges into clean, efficient, and user-friendly solutions. My goal is to build websites that not only look great, but also provide seamless and engaging interactions for every user.


I am actively seeking opportunities to contribute my skills to other innovative web projects. Feel free to explore my portfolio and connect with me to discuss how I can help you bring your next vision to life.
